About Me

I am an adaptable software engineer who loves the ever-changing programming landscape and learning new tools to create useful applications for others.

In my previous role as a pastry chef and baker, I have been able to see through the lens of my experience. The number of transferrable skills I gained from 20 years in this industry are incomparable. I used to think I wanted to create desserts, but now I enjoy building full stack projects and watching them grow.

In my next role, I would love to be a mentor to others while simultaneously expanding my skill set and knowledge. I enjoy growth, making connections, and honing my skills. I am confident that the skills I have acquired throughout my life will be a valuable asset to any company.
